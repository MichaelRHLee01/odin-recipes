Italian Chicken Marinade Recipe

Description

This Italian dressing chicken marinade is a super simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

  • Italian-style salad dressing
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Boneless chicken breast halves
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish
  2. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate
  4. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess
  5. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink
